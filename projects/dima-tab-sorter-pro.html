Overview

Tab Sorter Pro started as a personal fix for a real problem: too many open tabs, no structure, constant switching. It runs on both Chrome and Edge. The core idea is simple - hit a button and your tabs sort by domain and group automatically. No setup required to get started, but there's enough control underneath for people who want it.

What It Does

Auto-Sort

One click sorts all open tabs by domain and puts them into groups. Works on a full window of tabs in about a second.

Tab Groups

Creates color-coded groups automatically. You can rename and recolor them, or let the defaults do it.

Duplicate Cleanup

Finds and closes duplicate tabs. Useful when you open the same page twice without noticing.

Tab Search

Search across all open tabs by title or URL. Handy when you have 30 tabs open and need a specific one.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Sort and clean up without opening the popup. Assign shortcuts in Chrome or Edge settings.

Ungroup All

Flattens everything back to ungrouped tabs when you want a clean slate.
